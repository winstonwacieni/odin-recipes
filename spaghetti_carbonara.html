Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This is a classic Italian dish made with spaghetti, eggs, pancetta, parmesan cheese, and black pepper. The spaghetti is tossed with a creamy egg mixture and crispy pancetta for a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, in a separate bowl whisk together the eggs, parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  3. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with the pancetta. Toss to combine.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and add the egg mixture, stirring quickly to combine and avoid scrambling the eggs.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
