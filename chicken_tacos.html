Chicken Tacos

Description

These delicious chicken tacos are made with seasoned, grilled chicken, and topped with fresh lettuce, cheese, and your favorite toppings. Perfect for a quick and easy weeknight dinner or a fun weekend party.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Taco seasoning
  • Tortillas
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g. tomatoes, salsa, sour cream)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in the taco seasoning for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Grill the chicken on a barbecue or stovetop grill pan until fully cooked.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a pan or on a griddle.
  4. Assemble the tacos by placing the grilled chicken on the warm tortilla and topping with lettuce, cheese, and your desired toppings.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
