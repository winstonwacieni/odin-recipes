Recipe 3

Description

A delicious and flavorful dish that is sure to impress your dinner guests. This recipe is perfect for a special occasion or a cozy night in. Enjoy with your favorite sides and drinks.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of protein (chicken, beef, or tofu)
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 large carrot, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tsp of paprika
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • 1 tsp of chili powder
  • 1 tsp of dried oregano
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of black pepper
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup of chicken or vegetable broth

Steps

  1. In a large pan, heat a drizzle of oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Add the chopped carrots and red bell pepper to the pan, and cook for another 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
  3. Add the paprika, cumin, chili powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to the pan. Stir to combine with the vegetables.
  4. Add the protein to the pan and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes and broth to the pan. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer.
  6. Reduce heat to low, and let the dish cook for 10-15 minutes until the sauce has thickened and the flavors have melded together.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite sides and enjoy!
